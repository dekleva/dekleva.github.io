About me

Im a designer and creative coder from Slovenia, based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. I hold a BA in Visual Communications form the Faculty of Design in Ljubljana (2015) and a MA in Graphic Design from the Academy of Fine Arts and Design of university of Ljubljana (2020).

My practice explores the space of digital media with a use of typography, motion design and coding, primarily focused on the field of type and interaction design. Furthermore, I base my work on the knowledge from diverse fields such as media and language theory, critical and speculative design, semiotics and cognitive psychology to formulate deeper symbolic meanings and connotations hidden within visual language. I consider my work as visual problem-solving with an eye for technical peculiarities of a given medium, to communicate the clients intent to the target audience in the best way possible.

This is accumulated in my MA degree project titled “Subjective Typography”, in which I explored possibilities of using various input methods such as different APIs and sensors present in personal electronic devices to control interactive typographic elements in graphical interfaces and digital media.
