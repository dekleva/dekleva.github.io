About
Im a designer and creative coder from Slovenia, based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. I hold a MA in Graphic Design from the Academy of Fine Arts and Design of university of Ljubljana (2020). My practice explores the space of digital media with a use of typography, motion design and coding, primarily focused on the field of type and interaction design. Furthermore, I base my work on the knowledge from diverse fields such as media and language theory, critical and speculative design, semiotics and cognitive psychology to formulate deeper symbolic meanings and connotations hidden within visual language. I consider my work as visual problem-solving with an eye for technical peculiarities of a given medium, to communicate the clients intent to the target audience in the best way possible.
