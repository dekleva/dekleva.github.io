Kersnikova Institute

Visual Identity
Web Design

Kersnikova Institute is a non-profit and non-governmental cultural organisation, which serves as an institutional frame for three progressive venues: Kapelica Gallery, a world renown platform for contemporary investigative arts, the hacker space Rampa, where relations between society, science, technology and art are being reconsidered, and the inspirational laboratory BioTehna, which focuses on the artistic research of living systems.

Taking inspiration from the unique nature of the institute itself, the design of the website reflects their philosophy of combining science, culture and education. Our brief was to design a site that communicates the values of the institute while avoiding certain tropes of modern web design as much as possible. It employs the basic principle of a tree chart to represents the interconnectivity of information, concepts and activities. The structure of the site is flexible and always in motion. Each click expands and forms new connections, showing new articles, videos and imagery.
