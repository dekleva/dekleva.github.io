Academy of Music | University of Ljubljana

Art Direction
Visual Identity

The working relationship with the Ljubljana Academy of Music started with the simple task of producing a visual identity for the academic concert season along with posters and a booklet. We were given very little in terms of creative demands and spent quite some time coming up with a fitting concept. In the end we decided to showcase instruments from the symphonic orchestra mimicking different expressionist dance moves on colourful backgrounds bent in different shapes.
