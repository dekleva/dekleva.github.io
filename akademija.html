Academy of Music | University of Ljubljana

Art Direction
Visual Identity

Working relationship with the Academy of music Ljubljana started with a simple task to produce this year's concert season booklet. Just an update of a previous year with a slightly refreshed look. As we got the texts and other materials we found out that we don't have much to work with, because images were too low quality. While we were waiting for their reply about what to do, we started to render random music instruments to use as placeholder images in the design layout. While messing around with render setting, more realistic materials and adding different distortions we ended up liking the results and sending few of them to people at the Academy. The concept was to show various instrument from the symphonic orchestra on each spread in the booklet on colorful backgrounds bent in different shapes, mimicking different expressionist dance moves. They approved the mockups we sent them and gave us the green light to make it our way. The booklet was printed in bright red color with white harph highlighted with high gloss UV lack. After that we continued working together producing various printed materials and merch.
