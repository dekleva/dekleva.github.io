About me

Im a designer and creative coder from Slovenia based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. I hold a BA in Visual Communications from the Faculty of Design in Ljubljana (2015) and an MA in Graphic Design from the Academy of Fine Arts and Design at the University of Ljubljana (2020).
My practice explores the space of digital media through typography, motion design and coding and is primarily focused on the field of type and interaction design. Furthermore, I base my work on knowledge from diverse fields, such as media and language theory, critical and speculative design, semiotics and cognitive psychology, to formulate deeper symbolic meanings and connotations hidden within visual language. I consider my work as visual problem-solving with an eye for the technical peculiarities of a given medium to communicate the clients' intent to the target audience in the best way possible.
This is accumulated in my MA degree project titled “Subjective Typography”, in which I explored the possibilities of using various input methods, such as different APIs and sensors present in personal electronic devices, to control interactive typographic elements in graphical interfaces and digital media.
